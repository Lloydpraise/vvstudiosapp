VVStudios ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our application and our WhatsApp Business integration.

To provide our services, including WhatsApp messaging automation, we may collect the following:

  • Account Information: Phone number, business name, and email address provided during registration.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to:

4. Third-Party Sharing (Meta/WhatsApp)

Our services integrate with the WhatsApp Business API provided by Meta Platforms, Inc. Data required to send messages (such as phone numbers and message content) is shared with Meta to facilitate message delivery. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support.
